Source

Section 1 ¶

This section adds a new section to an existing chapter 74.13. Here is the modified chapter for context.

  1. [Empty]

    1. The department shall develop a home study training program which trains those not affiliated with the department or child-placing agencies to conduct home studies for the placement of children in foster care.

    2. The department may adopt rules as necessary to implement the home study training program.

  2. The department shall develop a process for those who have completed the home study training program to be able to conduct home studies for the department or child-placing agencies.

Section 2 ¶

This section adds a new section to an existing chapter 26.33. Here is the modified chapter for context.

  1. [Empty]

    1. The court shall develop a preadoption study training program which trains those not affiliated with the court to conduct preadoption studies.

    2. The court may adopt rules as necessary to implement the preadoption study training program.

  2. The court shall develop a process for those who have completed the preadoption study training program to be able to conduct preadoption studies for the court.
