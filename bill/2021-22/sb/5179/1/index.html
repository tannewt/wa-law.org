Section 1 ¶

This section modifies existing section 70.01.020. Here is the modified chapter for context.

  1. Any person of the age of 18 years or over shall be eligible to donate blood**, including donation through apheresis,** in any voluntary and noncompensatory blood program without the necessity of obtaining parental permission or authorization.

  2. Any person between the ages of 16 and 17 years old shall be eligible to donate blood, including donation through apheresis, in any voluntary and noncompensatory blood program after obtaining parental permission or authorization.
