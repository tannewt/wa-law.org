# SB 5711 - Private prison contracting
*Status: First reading, referred to Human Services, Reentry & Rehabilitation (Not Officially read and referred until adoption of Introduction report).* | [leg.wa.gov summary](https://app.leg.wa.gov/billsummary?BillNumber=5711&Year=2021)

Concerning repealing an unconstitutional ban on contracting with private prisons in the state of Washington pursuant to the 9th circuit ruling in The Geo Group v. Newsom.
