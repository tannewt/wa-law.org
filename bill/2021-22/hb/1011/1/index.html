House Bill 1011 ¶

Source

Section 1 ¶

This section adds a new section to an existing chapter 66.24. Here is the modified chapter for context.

  1. For any liquor license issued under this chapter that is subject to annual renewal and will expire on a date between the effective date of this section and March 1, 2021, the expiration of the license is extended and renewal is instead required by June 1, 2021. Upon renewal, the expiration date of the license may subsequently be prorated as necessary in accordance with chapter 19.02 RCW.

  2. This section expires June 30, 2023.

Section 2 ¶

This section adds a new section to an existing chapter 19.02. Here is the modified chapter for context.

  1. Liquor license renewals with the liquor and cannabis board are subject to section 1 of this act.

  2. This section expires June 30, 2023.
