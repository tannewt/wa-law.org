# House Bill 1977

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2021-22/Xml/Bills/House%20Bills/1977.xml)
## Section 1
> This section adds a new section to an existing chapter [42.56](/rcw/42_public_officers_and_agencies/42.56_public_records_act.md). Here is the [modified chapter](rcw/42_public_officers_and_agencies/42.56_public_records_act.md) for context.

Except as provided in RCW 42.56.250, all training curriculum and materials for any certified professional guardianship course or program of a public agency must be made available for copying and disclosure, and are not exempt from public disclosure.
