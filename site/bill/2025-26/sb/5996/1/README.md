# SB 5996 - Commercial shellfish fees

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2025-26/Pdf/Bills/Senate%20Bills/5996.pdf)

## Section 1
1. The department shall not increase fees above the amounts established as of June 30, 2025, for:

    a. A commercial shellfish harvest license;

    b. A shellstock shipper license;

    c. A shucker-packer plants license;

    d. An export certificate;

    e. Biotoxin testing; and

    f. Paralytic shellfish poisoning monitoring.

2. The department shall not impose any additional licensing or use fees for commercial shellfishers, shellstock shippers, and shucker-packer plants through June 30, 2027.
