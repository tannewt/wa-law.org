# SB 5950 - Celebrating Rosh Hashanah, Yom Kippur, Guru Nanak, Guru Gobind Singh, and Vaisakhi.

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2025-26/Pdf/Bills/Senate%20Bills/5950.pdf)

## Section 1
The legislature finds that many religious minorities are living in increased fear and isolation and are victims of increased incidents of bias and hate. Recognizing important holidays is one way to help communities feel seen, valued, and respected. At a time in which there is increased hate speech and more attacks on religious groups, saying that Washington is a home for all, and that we value and respect you and your community's contributions, is more important than ever. By expanding the list of nonlegal state holidays, the state helps recognize and celebrate the many faiths, heritages, and cultures of Washingtonians.
