SB 5379 - Parks & rec./interest arb. ¶

Source

Section 1 ¶

(1)(a) In order to maintain dedicated and uninterrupted services to the supervision of criminal offenders that are in state correctional facilities and on community supervision, it is the legislature's intent to grant certain employees of the department of corrections interest arbitration rights as an alternative means of settling disputes.
