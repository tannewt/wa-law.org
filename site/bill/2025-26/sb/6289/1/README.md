# SB 6289 - Economic dev. strategic plan

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2025-26/Pdf/Bills/Senate%20Bills/6289.pdf)

## Section 1
The department shall develop a statewide economic development and competitiveness strategic plan. At a minimum, the plan shall establish goals and strategies for state-supported economic development activities in the state, utilizing analysis of economic and industry data. The plan shall be completed by November 1, 2026, and updated every two years thereafter. Completed plans must be delivered to the appropriate committees of the legislature.
