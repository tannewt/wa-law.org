# SB 5500 - Child care rate model

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2025-26/Pdf/Bills/Senate%20Bills/5500-S.E.pdf)

## Section 1
1. By June 1st of every even-numbered year, the department shall publish a current cost of quality child care study, a cost of quality child care study as defined by the early educator design team, and a market rate survey and submit the information to the relevant committees of the legislature in compliance with RCW 43.01.036.

2. For the purposes of this section,the following definitions apply:

    a. "Cost of quality child care study as defined by the early educator design team" means using a cost estimation model, developed in consultation with and recommended by the early educator design team to the department, that includes, at a minimum, fixed costs on the following factors:

        i. Staff salaries based on a living wage scale as identified by the early educator design team;

        ii. Benefits, including paid leave, retirement contributions by employers, and expenses related to offering discretionary benefits to staff, such as health and life insurance;

        iii. Family engagement activities, staff, or both;

        iv. Planning release time;

    v. Costs for educational materials and curriculum; and

    vi. Professional development supports; and

    b. "Current cost of quality child care study" means using a cost estimation model that includes, at a minimum, current costs on the following factors:

        i. Staff salaries;

        ii. Benefits;

        iii. Family engagement activities;

        iv. Planning release time;

    v. Costs for educational materials and curriculum; and

    vi. Professional development supports.
