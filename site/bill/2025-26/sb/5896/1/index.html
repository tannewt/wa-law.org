SB 5896 - Water quality pollution ¶

Source

Section 1 ¶

It shall be unlawful for any person to throw, drain, run, or otherwise discharge into any of the waters of this state, or to cause, permit or suffer to be thrown, run, drained, allowed to seep or otherwise discharged into such waters any organic or inorganic matter that shall cause pollution of such waters according to the determination of the department, as provided for in this chapter.
