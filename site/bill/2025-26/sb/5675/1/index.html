SB 5675 - Manufacturing/B&O tax ¶

Source

Section 1 ¶

  1. This chapter does not apply to manufacturing facilities or green manufacturing facilities.

  2. The definitions in this subsection apply throughout this section unless the context clearly requires otherwise.

    1. "Green manufacturing facility" means a manufacturing facility that has been certified by a state or nationally recognized organization that rates the sustainability of the manufacturing process.

    2. "Manufacturing facility" means a facility that is used to manufacture, as defined in RCW 82.04.120.

  3. This section expires January 1, 2036.

Section 2 ¶

RCW 82.32.808 does not apply to this act.
