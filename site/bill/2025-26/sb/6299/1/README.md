# SB 6299 - AI & K-12 instruction

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2025-26/Pdf/Bills/Senate%20Bills/6299.pdf)

## Section 1
Artificial intelligence may supplement or enhance instruction. Artificial intelligence may not replace certificated instructional staff or paraeducators.
