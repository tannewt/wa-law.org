# HB 2713 - Detention facilities B&O tax

[Source](http://lawfilesext.leg.wa.gov/biennium/2025-26/Pdf/Bills/House%20Bills/2713.pdf)

## Section 1
1. Beginning July 1, 2026, in addition to all other taxes imposed under this chapter, persons must pay a surcharge on Washington taxable income arising from operating a private detention facility as defined in RCW 70.395.020 if the person has annual Washington gross receipts in excess of $1,000,000.

2. The rate of the surcharge is one percent of taxable income attributable to the operation of a private detention facility.
