HB 2166 - Major religious holidays ¶

Source

Section 1 ¶

  1. The legislature recognizes the importance of accommodating diverse religious practices and acknowledges the cultural and historical significance of certain Christian and Jewish holidays celebrated by Washingtonians.

  2. The legislature finds that the recognitions in this act promote inclusivity and respect for diverse faith communities in Washington state.
