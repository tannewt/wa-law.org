9A.80.010 - Official misconduct. ¶

  1. A public servant is guilty of official misconduct if, with intent to obtain a benefit or to deprive another person of a lawful right or privilege:

    1. He or she intentionally commits an unauthorized act under color of law; or

    2. He or she intentionally refrains from performing a duty imposed upon him or her by law.

  2. Official misconduct is a gross misdemeanor.

[ 2011 c 336 § 408; 1975-'76 2nd ex.s. c 38 § 17; 1975 1st ex.s. c 260 § 9A.80.010; ]
