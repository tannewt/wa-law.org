81.01.010 - Adoption of provisions of chapter 80.01 RCW. ¶

The provisions of chapter 80.01 RCW, as now or hereafter amended, apply to Title 81 RCW as fully as though they were set forth herein.

[ 1961 c 14 § 81.01.010; ]
